Privacy Policy – MyTempo
Last updated: January 2026

MyTempo (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is owned and operated by Alpha Architect Performance LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your information is collected, used, and protected.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the MyTempo mobile application (“App”).

By using MyTempo, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect
Information You Provide
When you use MyTempo, you may voluntarily provide the following information:
Account information (email address, login credentials)
Authentication data via:
Apple Sign In
Google Sign In
Email and password
Fitness data (workouts, exercises, sets, reps, weights)
Nutrition data (meals, calories, macronutrients, micronutrients)
Progress data (body weight, body fat percentage, goals, achievements)
Any information you choose to submit within the app

Automatically Collected Information
To ensure the app functions properly and improves over time, MyTempo may automatically collect limited technical data, including:
Device type and operating system
App usage data (feature interactions, session duration)
Performance and crash data
This information is used strictly for app functionality, analytics, and performance optimization.

How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
Create and manage your user account
Provide workout, nutrition, and progress tracking features
Personalize your experience within the app
Generate summaries, insights, and achievements
Improve app reliability, usability, and performance
Manage subscriptions and purchases
MyTempo does not sell your personal data.

Health & Fitness Data
MyTempo collects fitness- and nutrition-related data solely for personal tracking and progress visualization.
This data is not shared with advertisers
This data is not used for medical diagnosis or treatment
MyTempo is not a medical or healthcare application
You control the information you enter and can modify or delete it at any time.
