MyTempo Support We’re here to help you keep moving forward. Whether you’re experiencing a technical issue, have a billing question, or need help understanding how something works, the MyTempo support team is ready to assist. How to Contact Us Email: mytempocustomerservice@gmail.com When reaching out, please include: The email associated with your MyTempo account A brief description of the issue or question Screenshots (if relevant) This helps us resolve your request as quickly as possible. Technical Issues Contact us if you’re experiencing: Trouble signing in (Apple, Google, or email/password) App crashes, freezing, or performance issues Workouts, nutrition logs, or progress not syncing correctly Features not loading or behaving as expected We’ll help troubleshoot and get you back on track. Data & Progress Questions If something doesn’t look right, we’re happy to help clarify or fix it: Missing workouts or meals Progress metrics not updating Questions about how summaries or statistics are calculated Help understanding tracking or logging features MyTempo is built around clarity, and support is part of that experience. Billing & Subscriptions Reach out for help with: Subscription charges Free trials or plan changes Cancelations Refund requests Purchase access issues We aim to resolve billing-related questions quickly and fairly. Account & Privacy Requests You can contact us for: Account deletion requests Data access or privacy questions Changes to your sign-in method Email updates We take privacy seriously and handle these requests promptly. Feedback & Feature Requests We actively listen to our users. If you have: Feature ideas Suggestions for improving workouts or nutrition tracking Feedback on your overall app experience Send it our way. MyTempo improves through real user input. Response Time We typically respond within 1–2 business days. Our Philosophy MyTempo is built around one simple idea: Progress at your pace. Support isn’t about rushing you — it’s about helping you continue.
